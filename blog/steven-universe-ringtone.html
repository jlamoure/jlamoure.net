Sweet Steven Universe Ringtone with the Sonic Pi software

07 Dec 2017

This is a very simple construction using the Sonic Pi syntax to mimic Steven’s own ringtone for Season 2, Episode 1, song/episode title “Full Disclosure.” I wanted it as my ringtone, so I had to make it up myself.

Here’s the ringtone itself in .mp3!

Here’s the Sonic Pi code, which will play the ringtone forever:

use_synth :fm # select instrument
use_bpm 100 # set beats per minute
in_thread do
  loop do
    with_fx :tremolo, phase:0.25 do
      #
      play 94, release:0.5
      sleep 0.5
      play 103, release:0.75
      sleep 0.5
      play 101, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 98, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 99, release:0.5
      sleep 2.75
      #
      play 94, release:0.5
      sleep 0.5
      play 103, release:0.75
      sleep 0.5
      play 101, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 98, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 98, release:0.5
      sleep 2.75
      #
      play 94, release:0.5
      sleep 0.5
      play 103, release:0.75
      sleep 0.5
      play 101, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 98, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 99, release:0.5
      sleep 2.75
      #
      play 94, release:0.5
      sleep 0.5
      play 103, release:0.75
      sleep 0.5
      play 101, release:0.5
      sleep 0.25
      play 98, release:0.5
      sleep 4.75
    end
  end
end

I used the recording function in Sonic Pi to get a playable file.
