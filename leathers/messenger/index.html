The Messenger Bag

This is my messenger bag, each handmade.

Full grain leather means you get the full thickness of the hide.

The bag is a single piece of leather interwoven with the shoulder strap. This design is completely stitchless, highly aesthetic, and makes for a longer lifetime by relying on the strength of full grain leather.

The twist lock hardware is marine-grade and stays closed when closed.

The buckle is stainless steel.
